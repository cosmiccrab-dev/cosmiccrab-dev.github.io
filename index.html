Features

Engaging Story

Follow the story of a shipwrecked crew trapped in orbit of an uncharted world. Journey to the surface to repair your ship, study wildlife, and uncover secrets.

RTS Management

Coordinate your army of Sprouts to accomplish amazing feats. Convert any creature you defeat into new and diverse types of Sprouts.

Immersive World

Descend into several unique areas with real time simulated ecosystems. Watch predators hunt prey and find your place in the food chain.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Distant Planet?

The Distant Planet is an in-development game that fuses aspects of RTS, exploration, and action games. Command an army of animal-plant hybrid "Sprout" creatures to explore, fight off and study dangerous wildlife, invent and discover new technologies, and repair your stranded research vessel.

Where can I play? 

The Distant Planet is still under development and isn't playable yet. When the game is ready it will be available on Steam. We are only targeting Windows PC for release at this time. (We would love to port to other platforms post release however!)

How can I follow development?

You can join our Discord, follow us on X (Twitter), and wishlist us on Steam to be kept up to date and share your thoughts.

What is the release date?

To be announced, wishlist and follow on Steam and join our Discord server to be notified.
