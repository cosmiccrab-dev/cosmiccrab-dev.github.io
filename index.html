Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Distant Planet?

The Distant Planet is an in-development game in the newly emerging "Dandori" genre. Command an army of small "Minsprout" creatures to explore, fight off dangerous predators, and repair your space ship that's stranded in orbit.

How can I join the adventure?

The game is still under development and isn't playable yet, but you can join our discord, follow us on twitter, and wishlist us on steam to be kept up to date and join us for the ride.

Is The Distant Planet free to play?

The distant planet will be sold on Steam (and possibly other platforms!)

Can I play The Distant Planet on consoles?

The Distant Planet is not planned to release on consoles at this time, but it's definitely something we would like to do.

Who is behind The Distant Planet?

The Distant Planet is made by a small team of passionate developers in their free time.
